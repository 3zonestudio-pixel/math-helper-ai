How It Works

Three simple steps to understanding any math problem.

1

Capture or Type

Use the camera to scan a math problem, or type it directly. The app supports handwritten and printed text.

2

Get the Solution

The app first tries local solvers (arithmetic, algebra, calculus). For complex problems, it can call your configured AI API.

3

Learn & Review

Read through step-by-step explanations, save problems to your history, and mark favorites for later review.
